# Design

## Theme

Light, field-facing brand site with a darkened photographic hero. The physical
scene is a grower or operator checking irrigation decisions in daylight before
field work, so the interface should feel clear, practical, and readable.

## Color

Use Aradina green as a deliberate accent against warm stone surfaces and tinted
neutral text. Avoid saturated purple, blue SaaS gradients, pure black, and pure
white as dominant design moves.

## Typography

Use the existing system sans stack for now. Rely on scale, weight, line length,
and sentence-level copy discipline rather than decorative type.

## Layout

Prefer asymmetric marketing sections, strong left alignment, generous section
spacing, and dense operational groupings where the content is practical. Avoid
repeating centered headline plus identical card grids.

## Components

- Navigation: compact, sticky, clear destination links.
- Hero: photographic, product-specific, no decorative metric tiles.
- Feature areas: clickable, but visually varied by content weight.
- Pricing: utility-first controls with clear billing feedback.
- Contact: two-column pilot planning flow with direct contact details.

## Motion

Use short hover and focus transitions only. Avoid bounce, elastic movement, and
large decorative entrance animation.
