# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Growers, farm operators, and agricultural business owners in Egypt and the
broader MENA region who need clearer irrigation decisions from field data.

## Product Purpose

Aradina presents a smart irrigation offering that combines soil sensing,
analytics, and deployment support. The website should make the product feel
field-tested, operationally practical, and credible enough to start a pilot.

## Brand Personality

Grounded, technical, and careful. The tone should feel close to real farming
work, not like a generic AI or SaaS template.

## Anti-references

Avoid generic gradient SaaS pages, identical icon-card grids, hero statistic
templates, nested cards, vague automation claims, and decorative glass panels.

## Design Principles

- Show field reality before abstract technology.
- Use product and irrigation nouns, not startup filler.
- Make the buying path obvious: understand, estimate, contact.
- Keep the interface restrained, but not timid.
- Let spacing, imagery, and copy carry trust.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA contrast, visible focus states, logical keyboard order, readable
mobile text, and motion that remains subtle enough for reduced-motion users.
